Pancakes recipe

Description

Pancakes are a classic breakfast dish known for their soft, fluffy texture and mild sweetness. They are quick to prepare and can be served with a variety of toppings such as maple syrup, fresh fruit, honey, or butter. This simple pancake recipe uses basic ingredients commonly found in most kitchens. The result is a stack of warm, golden pancakes that are ideal for breakfast or brunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 ½ teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ¼ cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly greased frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour about ¼ cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip the pancake.
  7. Cook the other side until golden brown. Serve warm with syrup, fruit, or butter.
