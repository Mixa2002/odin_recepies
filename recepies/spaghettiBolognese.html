Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

Spaghetti Bolognese is a traditional Italian pasta dish made with a rich meat-based tomato sauce. The sauce is slow-cooked with ground meat, tomatoes, and aromatic vegetables, resulting in a deep and flavorful meal. This dish is popular around the world because it is filling, comforting, and relatively simple to prepare. It is commonly served with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and sauté until softened.
  3. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks.
  4. Stir in the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for 20-30 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  5. Serve the Bolognese sauce over the cooked spaghetti and top with grated Parmesan cheese.
