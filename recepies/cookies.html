Cookies recipe

Description

These classic homemade chocolate chip cookies are soft on the inside, slightly crisp on the outside, and filled with rich chocolate flavor. They are simple to prepare and require only common baking ingredients, making them an ideal dessert for beginners and experienced bakers alike. The cookies bake quickly and can be served warm with melted chocolate chips or stored for later as a sweet snack. Their balanced texture and flavor make them one of the most popular homemade treats.

Ingredients

  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter and sugars until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  6. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop rounded tablespoons of dough onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 9-11 minutes, or until golden brown.
  9. Cool on the baking sheet for 2 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
